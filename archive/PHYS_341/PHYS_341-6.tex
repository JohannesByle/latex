$v(0)=\frac{kv_{ex}}{m}(\frac{m}{b}+C)=0$\\
$C=-\frac{m}{b}$\\

$v=\frac{kv_{ex}}{b}(1-e^{-\frac{b}{m}t})$\\
$v=\frac{kv_{ex}}{b}(1-(\frac{m}{m_0})^{\frac{b}{m}})$\\
